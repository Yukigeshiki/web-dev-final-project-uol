About Our Group

We are a local succulent plant enthusiasts group. We aim to help those who, like us, have found pure bliss in the growth and propagation of succulent plants. In our articles section we've tried to include a little for everyone; from tips and tricks to keep your plants healthy, to tried and tested propagation methods for those aiming to share their plants with others. Also take a look at our events section to find out when our next get together is, we're always happy to see shiny new faces!
